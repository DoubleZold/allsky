Sky quality meter

Measures the brightness of the night sky in magnitudes per square arcsecond

Instrument: Unihedron SQM LE #4220
Filter: HOYA CM-500 Sensor field of view: 20

Note: this sensor is not working in a daytime which causes "drops" on the graphs between observation nights!
